Welcome to my homepage!


Born and brought up in Dharmavaram, famous for its "Silk sarees". Received my B.S. in ICT from DA-IICT in India, M.S. in Computer Engineering and Ph.D. in Cloud Computing from the University of Florida. I worked as a Research Assistant with Prof. Janise McNair and was a part of Wireless and Mobile Laboratory, and Small Satellite Design Club at University of Florida. I co-founded Edusoftware under the guidance of Prof. Prabhat Ranjan. My interests include Cloud Computing, Big Data, Small Satellites, Open Source, Distributed Systems and Crypto Currencies.

Education


Did my schooling from Sri Nagarjuna Vidhyalayam in Dharmavaram and then moved to Nalanda, Vijayawada for XI and XII in MPC. I did my undergrad in the field of Information and Communication Technology from DA-IICT and worked there for an year as research engineer under the guidance of Prof. Prabhat Ranjan. Received my M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering in Dec 2010 and Ph.D. in in Cloud Computing Dec 2013 from University of Florida.
